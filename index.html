Objectives

Central Theme

This connect is driven by a central theme which the projects, assignments, and field trips are centered around. Last year the centeral theme was centered around water which allowed students to create projects based on local and/or gobal issues/ideas around this theme.

Professional Enviroment

While in the professional learning community you will be in a professional enviroment.

Bring Your Friends

Imagine a connect with your buddies, wouldn't that be great? Well, you don't have to dream any longer. Get your friends to sign up for the professional learning community.

Projects

Rather than do 20 hours of service, you work on a project of your choice that is based on the central theme. These projects can be done in alone or in a group. This project will also be feature in the Itineris Journal.
